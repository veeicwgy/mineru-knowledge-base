CDN URL Patterns for LobeHub Icons
====================================

SVG CDN (unpkg):
https://unpkg.com/@lobehub/icons-static-svg@latest/icons/[ICON_SLUG].svg

SVG CDN (aliyun/npmmirror):
https://registry.npmmirror.com/@lobehub/icons-static-svg/latest/files/icons/[ICON_SLUG].svg

PNG CDN (unpkg):
Dark: https://unpkg.com/@lobehub/icons-static-png@latest/dark/[ICON_SLUG].png
Light: https://unpkg.com/@lobehub/icons-static-png@latest/light/[ICON_SLUG].png

PNG CDN (aliyun/npmmirror):
Dark: https://registry.npmmirror.com/@lobehub/icons-static-png/latest/files/dark/[ICON_SLUG].png
Light: https://registry.npmmirror.com/@lobehub/icons-static-png/latest/files/light/[ICON_SLUG].png
